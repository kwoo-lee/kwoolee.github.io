This is the first post on the blog. The plan is to write here regularly about things I'm building, reading, or learning — mostly software, systems, and the bits in between.

## Why a new blog

A lot of what I work on lives in scratch files, Notion, and chat threads. It's hard to come back to and harder to share. Writing it up in public forces a clearer second pass, and gives me something to point at later.

## What to expect

Short notes when I learn something worth remembering. Longer write-ups when a problem deserves a few thousand words. Occasional project announcements. No fixed schedule.
